Confirm Stripe Account Information
If your team plans to collect fees online, it is essential to confirm that your Stripe account information is current and active. You may directly access your Stripe account through Settings > Stripe Account.

Confirm Your Team's Charge & Payout Status
Confirm Your Team's Charge & Payout Status
After updating your Stripe account, navigate to the Stripe Account Page in your SwimTopia settings to confirm that your team account is set up to accept payments and has payouts enabled. If you notice a red box next to Accepting Charges or Payouts, click View Stripe Account to access your Stripe dashboard. 

Do not wait to check this until right before opening registration. It may take some time to gather the required information.
